How it works:

Rolling Strategy (TLT-style): Holds bonds with 20-30 years remaining. Initially distributed evenly: bonds with 21y, 22y, ..., 30y remaining. Each year, bonds that drop below 20y are sold and new 30y bonds are bought at current market rate. Coupons reinvested at market price.

Fixed Strategy: Buy bonds at initial rate and hold to maturity with annual compounding.

Interest Rate Model: FFR follows mean-reverting random walk toward end FFR. 30y rate derived from FFR changes with beta=0.3 (long rates less sensitive to short-term changes).
